## ⚡ Who We Are

VeloceAI is an AI infrastructure and solutions company building the full stack of intelligent systems — from sovereign edge devices to enterprise cloud platforms. We believe powerful AI should be accessible, private, and deployable anywhere.

We ship at the intersection of **edge computing**, **large language models**, **enterprise data pipelines**, and **agentic AI** — fast, compliant, and built for real-world constraints.
